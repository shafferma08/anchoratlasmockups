REAL ESTATE AGENT CONCIERGE

Elevating the buying and selling experience. We partner with agents and homeowners to ensure every property puts its best foot forward.

Listing & Sales Support

  • Home Staging: Transforming spaces to appeal to buyers and maximize value.
  • Exterior Staging & Preparation: First impressions matter.
  • Listing Preparation: Ensuring the property is photo-ready and pristine.
  • Decluttering & Organizing: Clearing and arranging spaces to show their full potential.
  • Home Watch: Monitoring vacant properties for safety.
  • Post Purchase Assistance: Helping new owners settle in seamlessly.

Logistics & Coordination

  • Moving & Relocation Coordination: Managing movers, packing, and logistics.
  • Utility Hook Up: Setting up water, power, and internet before arrival.
  • Furniture Delivery: Coordinating / receiving shipments so you don't have to wait.

Finishing Touches
