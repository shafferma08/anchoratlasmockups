Meet Wendy Bailey Chaves

CULTIVATING A LEGACY OF
SERVICE AND CURATED LIVING

Wendy Bailey Chaves is a trusted real estate professional and concierge service provider dedicated to helping clients navigate life’s transitions with confidence, ease, and exceptional support. With more than 35 years of experience spanning hospitality, event planning, public service, property management, and client-focused operations, Wendy has built a reputation for anticipating needs, managing details flawlessly, and delivering a seamless experience from start to finish.

As a licensed real estate professional, Wendy specializes in providing comprehensive listing concierge services that help real estate agents and homeowners maximize property value, enhance market appeal, and simplify the listing process. From home staging coordination and curb appeal enhancements to vendor management, move preparation, and closing support, she serves as an extension of the agent’s team, ensuring every detail is handled with professionalism and care.

Wendy is also passionate about assisting seniors and their families through the often-overwhelming process of transitioning to the next chapter of life. Through personalized senior living transition services, she provides compassionate guidance with downsizing, organizing, move coordination, estate sale planning, home preparation, and relocation logistics, helping families navigate change with dignity and peace of mind.

Known for her discretion, attention to detail, and white-glove approach to service, Wendy combines practical expertise with genuine compassion to create tailored solutions for each client. Whether supporting a real estate listing, coordinating a major move, or helping a family through a senior transition, Wendy is committed to reducing stress, simplifying complex logistics, and delivering an unparalleled level of service every step of the way.
